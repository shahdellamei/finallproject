About

In 2006, at the age of 12 years old we were taught how to analyze and make critical assessments on movies that we watched at the Cairo Film Festival for children and that is how I found myself sitting in a jury seat recurrently for the past 8 years.

Being part of this whole project was a surreal experience; and this love of being able to deliver something of any meaning to the public was what made me interested in this field of study. This is perhaps the most important reason behind my interest in Journalism.
